What is miiiCasa Open Platform?

The miiiCasa is to offer a easy, private, safeguarded place to share your life. Only your family or close friends are invited. Everyone keeps the digital collection at home and maintains overall control of them. The birth party photos, family trip videos, home music library or the family health information all could be found here easily and quickly. Everyone can play, share or interact without any privacy concern, just like a private home party. In addition, the intuitive interface is especially designed for the elder people or those without any technical background. We hope all household with any ages can truly enjoy themselves here.
The miiiCasa platform offers access to that corpus of data, via our APIs. Each API represents a facet of miiiCasa, and allows developers to build upon and extend their applications in new and creative ways. It’s important to note that the miiiCasa APIs are constantly evolving, and developing on the miiiCasa Platform is not a one-off event.
